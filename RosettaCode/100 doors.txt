■ HundredDoors 
  § static
    open⦂   boolean: true
    closed⦂ boolean: false
    doors⦂  boolean[1+100] · all initially closed

    ▶ main
    ⏺ args⦂ String[]
      ∀ pass ∈ 1‥100
        ∀ visited ∈ pass‥100 by pass
          toggle the doors, visited
      ∀ door #i ∈ doors⦂ boolean
        if the door is open
           System.out.printf "Door #%d is open.%n", i

    ▶ toggle
    ⏺ the door⦂ boolean[]
    ⏺ visited⦂ int
      if the door[visited] is closed
         let the door[visited] be open
      else
         let the door[visited] be closed
